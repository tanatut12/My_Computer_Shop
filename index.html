Keyboard

A keyboard is a flat device with buttons that you press to type letters, numbers, and symbols into a computer. It's like a typewriter, but for electronic devices. There are also special keys that can perform actions like copying, pasting, and taking screenshots.

Mouse

A computer mouse is a small handheld device that you move on a flat surface to control the cursor on your computer screen. The movement of the mouse is translated into movement of the cursor, allowing you to point to and interact with things on the screen. Most mice have at least two buttons for clicking and selecting items. Gaming mice often have additional buttons and features for more precise control and customization.

Headphone

Headphones are a pair of small speakers that you wear over or around your ears. They allow you to listen to audio privately, without disturbing others. Headphones come in wired and wireless varieties, and gaming headphones often have features like comfortable padding, noise cancellation, and a microphone for communication.
